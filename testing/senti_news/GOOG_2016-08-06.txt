Technology is now so pervasive in business that it might be time to call G.E. and Johnson &amp; Johnson tech stocks, not just the Silicon Valley stars.
Jeff Sommer Strategies column examines implications of fact that four most valuable companies in Standard & Poor's 500-stock index at close of trading on August 1 were Apple, Google, Microsoft and Amazon, all of which are tech companies; notes that tech as concept may not be useful for understanding market, as almost every company is tech company in one sense or another.
